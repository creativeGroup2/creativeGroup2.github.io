Water Retention Ability

Problems Solved

  • More Efficient Design / Lower Costs

    This windmill is smaller than a traditional windmill as the design captures wind energy in nearly all weather conditions. This pushes the costs down

  • Horizontal Design

    Our vertical axis design unlike the horizontal axis design is free from gyroscopic precission which is the major cause of breakdown of horizontal axis windmills in turbulent weather

  • Water Retention

    Our windmill can condense upto 1000 lit. of water a day, we intend to harness this water and use it for irrigation with our condensation unit

  • Sleek Design

    Concave blades and a smaller horizontal span allow for a low-cost windmill with more aerodynamic features and electricity generation

  • Solar Panels

    The condensation unit is also self sustainable with the help of solar panels

  • Funnel

    By the equation of continuity the wind exiting the funnel will have a higher speed and thus, we can break the Betz limit which states that only 59.3% of the Kinetic Energy of the wind can be used to spin the turbine
